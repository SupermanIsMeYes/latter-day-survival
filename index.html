About Me

My Name is Bryant. I served my mission in Santa Rosa CA, as well as Quetzaltenango Guatemala. During my time as a missionary, I love studying and would spend a lot of my extra time studying the second coming and other doctrine about the end of the world. I am currently a college student at BYUI studying software engineering. I created this website so that members of the Church of Jesus Christ of latter-day Saints as well as non-members can see prophecies of the end of the world and spiritually prepare for what is coming. I also am providing a way to help others find easy and inexpensive ways of preparing themselves physically for the catastrophes that are to come. I love Jesus Christ and I'm not fearful of the end because I know he has a plan.
