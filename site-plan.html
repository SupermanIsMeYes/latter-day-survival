Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a place where people can go and learn about how they can prepare physically and spiritually for the second coming. Focusing on food prep, wildernesss survival and spiritual survival.

Audience

Mostly members of the church but also non-members that are into prepping

Why will people come to your site?

My site will be a place where members of the church can come and find doctrine on the second coming as well as strategies to physically prepare for the second coming of Jesus Christ as well as spiritually.

What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them?

My site will answer questions on doctrine of the second coming. As well as how they can prepare physically for the second coming like how they can best make cheap food storage or what items should they have in case of emergency.

What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site?

My site is mostly informational, but it will help them understand how they can be physically and spiritually prepared for the end of the world. it will also teach them what activities will allow them to be so prepared.

What information is necessary to complete any actions your site offers?

The information necessary for my site will be the doctrine on the second coming including scriptures, teachings of prophets, and other sources. My site will also need the ability to show how you can physically be prepared for the second coming and follow all the guidance of the prophets.

Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions?

My visitors will come to my site instead of others because it will have high quality information that will allow them to make decisions in a way that will affect them for now and in eternity. My site will also have current events that line up with prophecies that many people will find very interesting and want to check up on what I post regularly. It will also have ways to achieve food independence cheaply and on a budget.
